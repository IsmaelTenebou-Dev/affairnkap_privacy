Account Deletion Request

AffairNkap – Manage Your Data Privacy

Overview

This page explains how users of AffairNkap can request the deletion of their account and associated personal data, in compliance with Google Play requirements and data protection regulations.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your AffairNkap account, please send an email to:

Email: support@affairnkap.com

Use the subject line:

Subject: Account Deletion Request – AffairNkap

In your message, include one of the following to help us identify your account:

  • The phone number associated with your account, or
  • The email address associated with your account

No additional information is required.

Send Deletion Request

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon verification of your request, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User profile information
  • Authentication identifiers (phone number or email)
  • Contribution and transaction history within the app
  • Any app-specific preferences linked to your account

Data That May Be Retained

The following data may be retained only if required by law or for security purposes:

  • Minimal server logs related to abuse prevention or fraud detection

If retained, such data is kept for a maximum of 30 days and is not used for any other purpose.

⏱️ Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days after receipt of the request.

⚠️ Important Notice

Once the deletion is completed, the account cannot be recovered.

Note: At this time, AffairNkap does not support partial data deletion without deleting the account. Users who wish to remove their data must request full account deletion.
