AffairNkap Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 15, 2025

AffairNkap ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and store information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use AffairNkap, we may collect the following:

a) Account Information

  • Phone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Full name
  • Profile photo (if uploaded)

b) Verification & KYC Documents

  • Identity documents (passport, national ID)
  • Selfie photos
  • Proof of address
  • Proof of income documents
  • Guarantor information

c) Payment & Financial Data

  • Mobile money transaction records
  • Payment history
  • Contribution amounts to Tontines

d) Audio & Communication Data

  • Voice messages sent in chats
  • Audio during one-to-one or group calls
  • Metadata: timestamps, sender/receiver IDs

e) Device & Usage Data

  • Device type, OS version
  • App usage data (which features you use)
  • IP address

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

User Verification & Security

  • To validate your identity and prevent fraud
  • To approve admins in Tontine groups

Tontine Operations

  • To manage contributions, beneficiaries, and payouts
  • To enable participation in group discussions and voice calls

Communication

  • To send notifications about contributions, approvals, or important updates
  • To enable voice messages and calls in the app

Compliance

  • To comply with legal obligations related to financial transactions

3. Audio and Voice Data Handling

  • Voice messages and group calls are encrypted and stored only as needed to deliver the service.
  • Audio data is shared only with intended recipients (chat participants or group members).
  • We do not use your audio for advertising or third-party profiling.

4. Sharing of Your Information

We may share your information:

  • With Group Members: for Tontine operations and discussions
  • With Admins: to verify contributions or KYC for group management
  • With Law Enforcement or Regulatory Authorities: if required by law
  • Service Providers: cloud storage or analytics services under strict confidentiality

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All sensitive data (KYC documents, payments, audio) is encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • We implement technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for service delivery, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and KYC documents
  • Update your profile information
  • Revoke microphone permissions at any time via device settings

7. Permissions Required by the App

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): to record voice messages and participate in one-to-one or group calls.
  • Camera (optional): if you add video calls in the future.
  • Storage Access: to upload KYC documents and download files.

The app will ask for these permissions at runtime, and you can deny them if you choose. Denying critical permissions may prevent certain features from functioning.

8. Children & Age Restrictions

AffairNkap is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we learn that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify users of material changes via the app or email. The "Effective Date" at the top will indicate the last update.
